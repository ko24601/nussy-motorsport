About The Team

The Vision

Founded by Marcel Hartwich, Nussy Motorsport is a competitive powerhouse in the sim racing esports arena. Sim racing isn't just a simulation — it's the ultimate frontier of modern motorsport, where engineering meets intuition and virtual asphalt demands the same respect as any physical track.

The Philosophy

From the tight chicanes of Monza to the grueling endurance of the Nürburgring, every driver under the Nussy banner carries a singular vision: to push beyond the limit. Success is about discipline in every practice lap and the courage to find the gap where others see a wall.
